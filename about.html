Meet the team

Rob Burton founded Platandia design studio in 2004. Rob personally selects all gemstones used in the making of the Platandia jewellery range to ensure the highest grade of colour, cut, brilliance and clarity. Over past decade the Platandia brand has grown substantially, through the support of its loyal client base, 5 in-house goldsmiths and designers, a diamond cutting works and gemstone lapidary as well as a luxury signature boutique - Platandia Jewellery boutique shares retail space together with their upmarket gift store Cape Gifts.

Our boutique

Platandia jewellery boutique is housed adjacent to the Cape Gifts Store in the foyer of the infamous Cape Grace Hotel along the West Quay at the V&A Waterfront. Cape Gifts is a family run boutique offering the discerning traveler excellent service and quality merchandise, by sourcing a unique variety of original artwork and curios from around Southern Africa.
